# Meeting Notes Skill

When the user asks to take meeting notes, use this structure:

## Template

- **Date**: {date}
- **Attendees**: {attendees}
- **Agenda**: Key topics discussed
- **Decisions**: What was decided
- **Action Items**: Who does what by when
